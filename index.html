About Us

Stephanie Albert and her team are licensed independent insurance brokers and financial advisors with over 25 years of experience. They specialize in life and living benefits. The team holds designations including Elder Planning Counsellor (EPC), Registered Health Underwriter (RHU), Certified Health Specialist (CHS), and Mental Health First Aid (MHFA).

Active members of the Independent Financial Brokers of Canada and MDRT, the team has been recognized for their professionalism and honesty, always prioritizing clients' best interests. Stephanie and her team are dedicated to community service and support various charities.
